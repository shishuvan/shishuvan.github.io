Important Instructions

INSTRUCTIONS WITH THE ADMISSION FORM (16-17) FOR READY REFERENCE OF THE PARENTS:

DOCUMENTS TO BE SUBMITTED WITH THE ADMISSION FORM:

  • The form will be accepted with the following attachments/enclosures :
    • Photocopy of Birth Certificate
    • Duly completed Medical History Form with photocopy of immunization record
    • Photocopies of Educational Certificates of Parent(s)
    • Caste Certificate in case of Minority
  • The original Birth Certificate should be produced as a proof of age at the time of submitting the application form.

ADMISSION PROCESS:

  • The applicants are required to strictly adhere to the age norms set by the school. The application will be rejected if the child is under or over age even by one day. Please refer to the age norms for each Std. given separately with this form).
  • Application form duly filled in should be submitted on the scheduled time. Late submission of application form will not be accepted / considered.
  • Incomplete or illegible forms will not be accepted / considered.
  • List of children selected for admission will be put up on the notice board on the scheduled date / time. No separate intimation will be sent to communicate the admission or non-admission of specific children.
  • On your ward being selected for admission, the fees should be paid in full strictly within the stipulated time, failing which the admission will be given to the other child in waiting without any intimation to you.
  • On the child being admitted to the school, it is compulsory to strictly adhere to the rules, regulations framed and disciplinary decisions / actions taken by the school.
  • In case, any information/particular furnished to seek admission to the School in the admission form or otherwise is found to be incorrect or misleading, the management reserves the right to cancel the admission, even after admmision is granted.
  • Please preserve the FORM NUMBER with you till the end of the admission process.

PLEASE NOTE:

  • FILLING UP OF THIS FORM DOES NOT GUARANTEE THE ADMISSION OF YOUR CHILD.
  • Admission is strictly on merit. "NO DONATIONS ARE EXPECTED / ACCEPTED
  • The school does not encourage any agents or management representatives for securing admissions. Please beware of any impostors. Dealings with such persons posing as agents / management representatives will be at your own risk and will not be binding on the school / management.
  • All decisions taken by the management concerning admissions will be final and indisputable.
  • ALL THE FEES PAID TO CONFIRM ADMISSION AND ANY FEES PAID THEREAFTER WILL NOT BE REFUNDED UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCES AND FOR ANY REASON.
