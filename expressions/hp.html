IDEA FROM AN EXTRA CLASS

an amazing experience wrote by Siddharth

We had this amazing creative writing session with Yashvi Gada. We were discussing on the topic of Harry Potter, when I came up with this crazy idea for an unimaginably super-duper crazy interview with You-Know-Who.(Harry Potter of course)

Guess what happened when Harry Potter came to India.Simple. He was literally drowned in a gigantic wave of paparazzi.Here is an extract from their conversation:-

Paparazzi: So what do you think of your name?

Harry Potter: Insulting. I mean, everybody says I should be making pots, because that is what a 'Potter' does.

Paparazzi: Ha.Ha. Tell me . What did you do on your first day in Hogwarts?

Harry Potter: I laughed. I thought they would turn me into those despicable hogs and infect me with revolting warts.

Paparazzi: What do you have to say about your ability to speak to snakes?

Harry Potter: It does come in handy. But when I first spoke to a snake, I knew the words, but not the meanings(not true in the book). I picked up random words and blabbered them like a maniac. When I finished my sentence, the snake laughed. Only I saw it. I figured out later that it meant-"I have potassium nitrate in my cow dung soup." I know, very funny. Hilarious even. But you really cannot blame me. When I spoke to another snake, it laughed harder than the previous one. I figured out later that it meant-" I have potassium nitrate in my donkey-dung filled diaper. Really I don't know what's up potassium nitrate and all sorts of dung.

Paparazzi DIES of laughter.

If you read the entire conversation, you'd be laughing your heads off, and I really do not want you to be kind of beheaded by ME, because I'm completely INNOCENT.
