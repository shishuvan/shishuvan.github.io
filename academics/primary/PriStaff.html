Staff

The Head of Department along with the class teachers works with environmental themes, weaving in reading, writing, speaking, laboratory work and mathematical proficiency and computer knowledge. Class teachers are assisted by specialists in the area of music, dance, art & craft, personality development, games, gymnastics, social service, field visits and library. Three Indian languages - Hindi, Marathi and Gujarati, remains the norm for the Primary.
