Rules And Regulations

GENERAL

  • Students need to be in school by 8.50 a.m. (Sharp). If a child comes in later than 8.50 am in pre-primary more than twice in a month, then he / she will be sent back home.
  • Use of the school bus is mandatory. Use of personal vehicles to drop the students is not allowed due to heavy traffic in the lane.
  • Students are not allowed to:
    • Wear or bring valuables to the school.
    • carry money to school.
    • apply nail paint / polish, tattoos
    • apply mehendi (Except with prior permission). (Students found with the above will not be allowed to attend school on that day.)
    • Students found with hair colour application will not be allowed to attend the school for a week.
  • Gifts and cash are strictly not to be given to any staff member including bus attendants, on any occasion.
  • Personal hygiene is important –
    • Please ensure that your sons/daughters nails are clipped.
    • Only short cropped hair is allowed for boys. Mushroom cuts and spiked hair cuts are not allowed.
    • For girls, hair should be neatly tied with black plain elastic bands or black clips. Hair till neck length should be tied into ponytail(s) and hair beyond the neck length should be neatly plaited.
    • A handkerchief should be sent in the child's pocket.
  • The school is not responsible if children misplace any of their belongings.
  • The students will not be allowed to make any calls during school hours.
  • If you wish to celebrate your sons/daughters birthday in class, please note that you may send only an eggless cake or an Indian sweet, which should be handed over to the school office by 10.15 a.m. (as parents are not allowed into classroom). No return gifts and sweets/chocolates can be sent for your child's classmates.
  • The school strongly discourages tuition and private classes for the children. The school expects you to co-operate in this regard.
  • A fine will be charged on late payment of school & bus fees.
  • Students who fail to submit their homework on time will be detained in school from 3.00 p.m. to 4.00 p.m
    • UNIFORM
  • During the lunch break, the students will have to eat their dry lunch or tiffin lunch in the school premises only. The students will collect the tiffins and drop them back to the designated place. Parents/helpers are not allowed to enter the school building during lunch hours.
  • School uniform comprises of a Khadi uniform, black socks, black shoes, a cap and a sports uniform. (Refer to footnote 1)
  • During the monsoon, it is compulsory for students to wear all weather shoes and carry a raincoat in his/her bag.
  • Students are to wear all Black shoes (Action Shoes, Code : Sr. No. A5, & TMS-743) for sports and rest of the year.
  • If the students' uniform does not meet the standards set by the school, they will be sent back home.
  • The Primary students must wear the Shishuvan sports uniform only on the days the students have a physical education/gymnastics period. If students fail to dress appropriately they will miss physical education/gymnastics.
  • Carrying khadi uniform with them to change into, after their sports/gym period, is mandatory for all primary students.

    • LEAVE AND ABSENCE
  • The attendance from the first working day of the school to the last working day of the school before vacation is mandatory. Any exception (for whatever reasons) would follow the school policy of parents rendering library services.
  • Students attendance for non-instructional working days is mandatory (Independence Day, Gandhi Jayanti and Republic Day) and on all school events including Fair, Sports Day, Annual Day (including rehearsals), field visits, outstations tours and Project Day. If the student is absent any of the above, the parents have to render library services.
  • Student absent from the school for a period of one month without permission will be struck off the school register. Re-admission into the school will be treated as a fresh application.
  • Leave of absence from school must be obtained a week prior by an application submitted in the prescribed Non-Attendance and Leave record in the diary. The same rules are applicable for half day leave. A student will not be allowed to leave during school hours unless she/he seeks prior permission, giving valid reasons.
Footnote 1
Soak the green khadi pant in salt water before the first wash. Wash the green pant separately to avoid staining. Do not dry any of the uniforms under direct sunlight. Do not use washing material that has high bleaching agent and avoid the use of hot water.

RULES FOR STUDENTS USING THE LIBRARY

  • The library is open for parents to come with their ward/child, on Saturdays, between 9.00 a.m. to 3.00 p.m. (Please check with the office for Library access during vacations.) Please bring your child's identification card / diary along. No eatables or water bottles will be allowed inside the Library. Silence should be maintained at all times. All bags except small money purses should be left at the entrance of the library.
  • Primary students will be issued books during their library period. Only one English / Hindi book will be issued per week for reading at home.
  • Please check your child's time table so that you can ask to see that book he / she has got from the school library for reading at home. If he/she has not got a book home, kindly ask your child about it or get in touch with the Librarian to find out if a book has been issued. Your child may have left the book in school or in the school bus.
  • The book should be returned on the next Library period, or re-issued if the student has not finished reading it. Please note that the book cannot be re-issued more than once.
  • If the book is not returned in time (including vacation time) , no new library book will be issued for another week until the fine of Rs.5/- per week has been paid.
  • Books have to be returned on the last class library day before every vacation.
  • Should the book be damaged or torn, 50% of the cost of book will have to be paid by the student. Each student will be responsible for their books.
  • In case any query parents can meet the librarian at 3.00 p.m. on Wednesdays.

PARENT PARTICIPATION

Student’s learning is optimised by a strong Home-School partnership. Hence parent’s participation forms a significant component in the Shishuvan curriculum. The school organises workshops for parents in relation to child development, pedagogy, career option and forms of parent support to enhance learning in their sons/daughters.

HENCE IT IS MANDATORY THAT BOTH PARENTS PARTICIPATE ACTIVELY IN:

  • All school events such as the School Fair, Sports Day, Annual Day, Project Day and Report Day.
  • Parent Teacher Meetings (PTM's). The student's progress will be made known to you at the PTM.
  • The field trips which is by rotation and mandatory.(Parents will be given the list of days and dates for the trips).

SCHOOL COMMUNICATION
  • The voluntary help form, the parent consent form and the medical report form must be filled in and submitted before end of 1st week.
  • All notices / circulars sent in the students diary must be signed by the parents on very next day.
  • Any queries about your son/daughter can be addressed to the class teachers only by way of a note in the diary.
  • If the student is not using the bus service on a particular day, a written note should be sent in the diary to the class teacher.
  • Meeting the Head of Department or any of the teachers requires a prior appointment, requested in writing, through the student’s diary.
  • Parents can avail of the school's counseling service by seeking an appointment with the counselor through the class teacher.
  • Cancellation of any of the appointments requires an intimation to the school office between 8.00 a.m. to 9.00 a.m.
  • Parents are expected to notify the school office and class teacher regarding any change in their address or telephone numbers to update our records.
  • Please use the suggestion box freely to drop your suggestion(s), placed outside the School office or in the grievance register in the office or email at primary@shishuvan.com or shishuvanschool@gmail.com
  • The 'Parents Sabha' will be held once a month. The class preparatory meeting will be held one week prior to the ‘Parents Sabha’. The schedule for the same will be sent later.
  • All decisions including some medical decisions, that affect the learning of the student (or due to lack of satisfaction with their academic progress) should be taken in consultation with the school. Any medication administered on the student must be known to the Head of Department, the concerned class teacher and counselor / remedial educator (if involved).
  • Parents will not be allowed to enter the school premises without a valid gate pass. Entry into the school premises for all parents, visitors and outsiders is restricted due to security reasons. Kindly do as instructed by the security personnel at the gate.
RIGHT OF ADMISSION
  • The application for a School Leaving Certificate must be made in writing by the parent or guardian to the School Office. No Leaving Certificate will be issued until all dues are cleared.
  • Management reserves the right to suspend any student from school or from specific school services if behaviour is found inappropriate. The final decision taken by the management would rest on consideration of parent, teacher and administrative viewpoints and would be binding upon the student. Thereafter no discussion would be entertained by the parents.
  • Any decision taken by the school for the welfare of the student and for school discipline will be binding on the parents.
  • Fees must be paid on fine failing which fine will be levied as per school norms. Please not fees once paid is not refunded.
