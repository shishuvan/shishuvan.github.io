Beyond Academia

What the hands do is what the brain needs in order to establish proper connections with the nervous system. Through the work of their hands, human beings have progressed and prospered. 

These sessions will focus on Creative arts with the aim of developing the child’s fine motor skills, gross motor skills, hand eye co-ordination, imagination and creativity.   

The activities would include hand work, exploring different forms of painting, candy stick fun, create a story with pop up live book, making your own puppets and build your own puppet show....and other such hands-on activities.

The sessions will be conducted by Ms. Hemal Chhadawa. She is a trained Waldorf Rudolf Steiner Educator since 10 years, She has been conducting the Creative Club for Shishuvan secondary students since last 6 years. She has been contributing on developing the handwork curriculum. She has also been conducting Rudolf Steiner Education course for Shishuvan parents since 3 years.
